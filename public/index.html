Add-Ons

Domain Registration

If you're establishing a website you will need a custom domain name. Domain names are usually purchased in one to three year terms. Once the domain name is purchased you will update your domain records to point traffic to your hosting provider. We can help you register your domain and update appropriate configuration settings required for website hosting.

Website Hosting

Web hosting is a critical element of running your website. Inexpensive websites paired with legacy web applications can lead to a poor user experience. We can help you establish and configure hosting on powerful platforms such as Amazon Web Services and Heroku. Scalability as your website scales is critical and finding a trusted hosting provider can make your break your users experience.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is an application that you embed into a website to identify how your users are interacting with your content. Would you like to know if users are getting to your contact form but then failing to send their contact information? Google Analytics can help provide data to make better design decisions. Google Analytics can provide you with visiter demographic information, how long they stayed on your website, what they looked at, and so much more to help you start building a profile of your website users.

Website Monitoring

Website monitoring is the process of testing and verifying that end-users can interact with your website. Website monitoring is critical as your website grows in popularity. On demand shoppers need website content available to them 24 hours a day 7 days a week. Website monitoring allows you to have confidence that your online presence is available at all times.

Our Process

Edocew is a web development studio, we rely on web designers or pre-built website designs. Why does this benefit you? Let us explain. When you decide to purchase a home you can decide to accomplish it in one of two ways:

  1. Hire an architect and build a custom home that is one of a kind.
  2. Buy a set of home plans that are already designed from a “design” firm or from a building contractor who’s already purchased them

Why would you want to work with pre-existing website design plans? First, it’s less expensive. A good web designer can charge from $1,500 to hundreds of thousands of dollars for a bespoke design. Pre-built website designs can usually be purchased for less than $100. Second, is time to value. Working with a designer takes time, and the longer it takes, the longer you’re delaying your time to value. Most small businesses are short on time and money, so we believe that working with the hundreds of established web designs available is the best use of your time and money.

1

Project Discovery and Scoping

We will establish a meeting where we will get to know each other and initiate our working relationship. Additionally we will discuss your project, including success criteria, desired outcomes, target customer profile, and overall budgets.

  • Web site requirements
  • Project timelines
  • Stakeholders involved
  • Services desired
  • Methodology overview
  • Review and delivery of design options
2

Proposal Generation and Presentation

After project discovery and scoping we will generate a proposal for you to review. We will establish a follow up meeting to review all elements of the engagement. The proposal will include the following information.

  • Project scope details including
    • Design choice
    • In scope services
    • Timelines
    • Hosting details
  • Pricing and payment terms
  • Billing milestones
  • Project assumptions
3

Website Prototype Generation

After receiving your content and images, or image selections are finalized, we will begin the prototype phase of your project. A no-code / low-code mockup will be created that includes all client provided verbiage and images. The following steps occur in this phase:

  • Initial prototype generation
  • Up to three (3) revisions of text, images, or site color
  • Final website mock-up in image or PDF format
  • Protype acceptance and sign off
4

Website Development and Testing

We take all of the individual graphic elements from the prototype and build your website. This includes both the desktop view and the mobile responsive views, ensuring that your site is accessible to as large an audience as possible. Additional steps taken during this step include:

  • Google Lighthouse testing to ensure proper search engine optimization (SEO) and speed
  • Mobile device testing including phones and tablets
  • The development website is hosted on our servers for your review
5

Add-on Configuration (Optional)

If you need additional help with web hosting or web related services, the following elements will be integrated after initial development efforts have been completed.

  • Website hosting and deployment
  • Google Analytics to track user engagement
  • Domain registration and configuration
  • Website monitoring
6

Website Handoff

This is when we conclude the project.

  • Bundle up source code in zip file and email to you
  • Final payment is made
7

Website Maintenance

Once your site is launched, that doesn’t mean that all the work is over. One way to bring repeat visitors to your site is to offer updated content on a regular basis. If you don’t feel comfortable running the upgrades yourself, and/or you prefer to better spend your time running your business instead of worrying about your website, We will be more than happy to continue working together with you to keep your website up to date.

MyPRs

This application is a mobile friendly website that tracks fitness personal records. A user can create an account and log into the application to record fitness goals. myPRs has three categories of movements: Lifts, Cardio and Skills. The application is already loaded with predefined movements in each category and the user can create new movements to add to their list. Within each specific movement, the user can add new 1 rep max logs, edit the log or delete it. I was inspired to create this project because I needed a way to track my own fitness personal records with a specific, easy to use application. There are many other beautiful fitness application on the market but they tend to get very busy with too many options and I just wanted a simple quick way to look at my person fitness records.

My focus on this project was to build the frontend in React. Node.js is being used for the backend and MongoDB for the database. I used Balsamiq to wireframe my design and lucid charts to map out my data. Postman was used to test all of my endpoints throughout development.

  • Reset Password

  • Contact Support
